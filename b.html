About us.

Coffee meets book... Gönül Kahvesi, aiming the worldwide mission to deliver Turkish coffee and espresso the value it deserves, with years of experience and innovative approach, was established in 2002 with a single store in Izmir. Today, with 78 stores in Turkey and abroad, setting the mission of the most recognised coffee chain of its business area, Gönül Kahvesi is closer to achieve its goals with unique products, concept stores and creative approach to the business. Gönül Kahvesi also promises to create a unique space where people can socialize with each other in a comfortable and relaxing environment while enjoying the best variety of coffees. Our vision is to make Gönül Kahvesi a worldwide brand with progressive, quality focused enterpreneur structure. Our mission is to qualify Turkish Coffee to be an indispensible brand in national and international market.
